Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MerchPA

How does the AI-powered analytics work? +
MerchPA's AI analyzes patterns in your field data to generate actionable insights. It tracks individual and team performance, identifies optimization opportunities, predicts high-potential stores, and recommends route improvements. The system learns from your data to provide increasingly accurate recommendations over time.
Does the mobile app work offline? +
Yes! The MerchPA mobile app is fully functional offline. Merchandisers can complete check-ins, take photos, fill out audit forms, and capture all necessary data without internet connection. Data automatically syncs when connectivity is restored.
How quickly can we get started? +
Most teams are fully operational within 1-2 weeks. We provide comprehensive onboarding, training materials, and dedicated support to ensure a smooth transition. Enterprise clients receive custom onboarding programs tailored to their needs.
Can MerchPA integrate with our existing systems? +
Absolutely. Our platform includes integration capabilities with major CRM and ERP systems including Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, and Microsoft Dynamics. We also provide API access for custom integrations.
